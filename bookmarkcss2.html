Tiger

Tiger is the biggest wild animal in the cat family, it is one of the strongest animals in the jungle. Tiger is a “Carnivorous” animal which means it is a flesh-eating animal; it hunts other animals for food. Tiger has a long and strong body. It has four legs, strong paws with sharp nails, and one tail.

kangaroo

Kangaroos can move backwards as well as forwards. The red kangaroos are the largest species. They are extremely strong and can be extremely dangerous. They are territorial and very protective of their babies.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth, with massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks123. They use their trunks for various purposes, such as picking up objects, trumpeting warnings, greeting other elephants, and drinking or bathing1.
